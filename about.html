VISION, MISSION & PRINCIPLES

VISION

We work towards a model of scholarship that connects people through enhanced knowledge to reshape the future of humanity.

MISSION

To provide scholarly and innovative solutions to problems facing communities and people through research, training and publishing.

PRINCIPLES

The following eight principles underpin the work of the RAL Institute to achieve our vision and mission:

  • Quality
    We always connect excellence to our image with the desire to achieve

  • Empowerment
    We strongly have faith in equal access to opportunities as an effective
    avenue for socially vulnwerable people to rise up social ladders if given
    a chance.

  • Truth and Reliability
    Our sole pursuit is Truth for we believe like William O. Douglas that
    âtruth is the proper and sufficient antagonist to errorâ and that truth

  • Transformative and ethical Leadership
    We trust in transformative and ethical leadership to drive the best
    out of each person towards creating a better world.

  • Freedom of thought and expression
    We strongly believe that man needs full freedom of thought and
    expression to realize his utmost capacities and become a cultured
    citizen of the national and world communities.

  • Human dignity
    Our whole work is driven by the urge to uplift human dignity, to
