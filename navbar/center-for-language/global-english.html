Global English

Become a near-native speaker with American or British accent


WHO IS THIS COURSE FOR?

Our Global English course is designed for junior and adult learners who would like to widen their skills and develop a good command of English. You are also in the right place if you start from scratch.



WHAT IS THIS COURSE ABOUT?

  • Methodical language education focusing on linguistic (listening, reading, speaking and writing) competences, interactive communication and discourse types
  • Effective communication strategies and techniques for different situations, settings and audiences
  • Integrated 21st-century information, technology, creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills into language instruction
  • Potential completion of 3 CEFR levels in just 12 months with our year-round program



HOW CAN OUR COURSE TRANSFORM YOUR ENGLISH SKILLS?

  • We pinpoint your English language problems
  • We provide reasearch-based solutions
  • We boost your vocabulary and grammar

  • We spot your mistakes and needs
  • We littearally upgrade your pronunciation
  • We help you create and install English language habits

HOW IS THE COURSE STRUCTURED?

The Global English is a 300-hour program that combines evening and day-time classes to run over 14 weeks with:

  • 108 hours of in-class courses
  • 32 hours of practical work in class
  • 150 hours of student’s personal practice and homework
  • 10 hours of educational outing/Intercation with a guest



THE CLASSES WE TEACH, THE EXAMS YOU CAN TAKE!

Our Global English program instills in you the "CAN-DOs" of each CEFR level and prepares you for the Cambridge English Qualifications, IELTS and TOEFL.

CERTIFICATIONS

Upon completion of each class level, you will receive:

  • our Certificate for the completed level
  • an ITTT-signed Certificate, internationally accredited
  • you will be able to take the Cambridge English Qualification exams like CPE, CAE, FCE, PET and KET.
